Common Questions

Is Shop Well really free?

Yes, completely free forever. No subscriptions, no premium tiers, no hidden costs. This project is built to help the chronic illness community, not to profit from it.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All product analysis happens locally on your device using Chrome's built-in AI. No data is sent to external servers. No account required. Your privacy is guaranteed.

What conditions are supported?

Currently: POTS (Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome), ME/CFS (Myalgic Encephalomyelitis/Chronic Fatigue Syndrome), and Celiac Disease. More conditions planned for future updates.

Which websites does it work on?

Currently supports Amazon.com and Walmart.com product pages. We're starting with the most popular shopping sites and will expand based on community feedback.

Do I need to enable Chrome AI?

Yes, you'll need Chrome 128+ with AI features enabled. Don't worry‚Äîwe provide step-by-step instructions during setup. It takes less than 2 minutes.

Is this medical advice?

No. Shop Well provides informational analysis only and is not a substitute for medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider for medical decisions.
